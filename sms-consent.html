SMS Consent Documentation

Last updated: March 11, 2026

This page documents how Austin Broyles ("Austin Broyles," "we," "us," or "our") collects consent to send SMS messages and describes the nature of those messages. It is provided for transparency and for regulatory compliance purposes.

About This Messaging Program

Austin Broyles uses an automated AI assistant platform to send SMS messages via the Twilio API on his behalf. Messages are programmatically triggered for transactional purposes, including scheduling coordination, appointment confirmations, and follow-up communications with known contacts. No bulk marketing or promotional messages are sent.

How Consent Is Collected

Contacts provide consent by voluntarily sharing their personal phone number with Austin Broyles directly — in person, via email, or during the normal course of an established personal or business relationship. At the time of sharing their number, contacts are informed verbally or in writing that they may receive SMS messages from Austin Broyles for scheduling, appointment confirmations, and related follow-up communications.

Consent is never obtained through purchased lists, scraped data, or unsolicited outreach. Each contact has a pre-existing relationship with Austin Broyles prior to receiving any messages.

Consent Language

The following disclosure is communicated to contacts at the time they provide their phone number:

"By providing your phone number, you consent to receive text messages from Austin Broyles for scheduling, appointment confirmations, and related follow-up communications. An automated messaging system may be used to send these messages on Austin Broyles's behalf. Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact austin.broyles@gmail.com."

Message Types

Messages sent under this program may include:

  • Appointment confirmations and reminders
  • Scheduling coordination and availability requests
  • Follow-up messages related to services or prior conversations
  • General coordination with contractors, service providers, and family members

Messages are not promotional, marketing-related, or solicitations of any kind.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on scheduling activity and the needs of individual contacts. Standard message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages from Austin Broyles at any time by replying STOP to any message. You will receive a confirmation that you have been unsubscribed and no further messages will be sent. To resume messages, reply START.

For help or questions, reply HELP to any message or contact austin.broyles@gmail.com.

Data Privacy

Mobile phone numbers and SMS opt-in consent collected by Austin Broyles are used solely for the messaging purpose described above. Mobile information, including phone numbers and opt-in consent data, will not be shared with, sold to, or transferred to any third party or affiliate for marketing, promotional, or any other purpose. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Contact

For questions about this consent documentation or our messaging practices, contact: austin.broyles@gmail.com
